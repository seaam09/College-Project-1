About the College

History • Mission • Vision

History

Dhaka Central Polytechnic Institute (DCPI) is a prominent private Polytechnic Institute, established in 2006 and affiliated with Bangladesh Technical Education Board under the Ministry of Education.

Mission

Technology is the language of modern civilization. Without command over technology - it is very difficult to be a competent nation in the world. DCPI believes in the power of technology and wish to control it by tech loving students. The institute has taken adequate measures to contribute in technical education sector keeping persistent effort.

Vision

In the fast altering context of techno-eco and social realities, the vision of Dhaka Central Polytechnic Institute (DCPI) was conceptualized in 2006 by a team of committed professionals. The Institute has been founded in 2006 with affiliation of Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB).

Academic Programs & Departments

Diploma courses • Departments

Diploma in Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, roads, and bridges. This program equips students with practical and theoretical skills for the development of modern infrastructure.

Diploma in Computer Science and Technology

Computer Science and Technology covers computer programming, hardware, networking, and software development. Graduates are skilled in solving technological problems and developing innovative digital solutions.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering focuses on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. Students learn to design, develop, and maintain electrical systems and equipment.

Diploma in Electronics Engineering

Electronics Engineering specializes in the design and development of electronic circuits, devices, and systems. The course prepares students for careers in automation, embedded systems, and modern electronics industries.

Diploma in Telecommunication Engineering

Telecommunication Engineering deals with the design, installation, and maintenance of communication systems. Students gain expertise in networking, satellite systems, and modern communication technologies.
